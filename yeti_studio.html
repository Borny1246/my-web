Our Story

Yeti Studio was set out in 2018 and formally established in 2021 by Prajwal
Shrestha and a small team with a clear vision to solve digital needs and
assist businesses in growing their online presence. From a very small
company, it grew into a full-service IT company and digital solutions provider
trusted by many SMEs and enterprise businesses.

The begining

Prajwal Shrestha, a step-up entrepreneur with a sharp eye for technology and
innovation, felt the rising need for any business to have an online presence.
He saw filling this gap by delivering end-to-end digital solutions that would
help businesses prosper in today’s digital world.

The Journey

Right from its inception, Venture Studio pegged itself on the principles of excellence and making a difference.

Learn together Grow together, these very hallmarks have
defined our journey.

We started as a small team of dedicated professionals who shared
Prajwal’s vision and passion for doing things differently.

We came together with a shared vision:
to deliver impactful, high-quality digital solutions that make a difference.
We have spent years honing our team, refining our craft, and expanding our services.
Now on board with the Yeti Studio are the full-service digital solutions including,
Mobile App Design & Development, Website Design & Development, UI/UX Design, Digital Marketing,
Content Writing, Graphic Design, Branding, e-commerce website development, and SEO Services.
We do provide a full service digital agency, approach organically to deliver all our
client’s digital requirements with wellness strategies for digital success.
